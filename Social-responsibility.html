Social responsibility!

Meeting the elders!

As events of life pass I gained a great deal of empathy towards the less fortunate, through one of my classes I had voiced my idea to go meet the elders who hadn't been outside for quite some time and are saddned by the ignored world. They could be depressed by being stuck within the four concrete walls of the elders home. And so we visited them with several snacks and gifts to enlighten their lives...

Interact Club of CIS

Even in smaller scales, I have enrolled with Interact Club in many of their projects volunteering to stay hours after school, to raise funds to visit orphanages. Satisfiable amount of fund was raised by sale of food and drinks during school matches but unfortunately all plans are being delayed due to the pandemic.

Robin Hood Army

I joined with some of my friends and partnered up with the local Robin Hood Army where we went hunting for the homless people and provided them with a meal and a chilling drink to brighten up their day. As they filled their stomachs with a smile on their faces and their prayers for our act of kindness made our day.

Car Wash!

Here too we had sacrificed a Sunday morning, our late hours of sleep and got up early for this event where with the help of my peers we got permission from the council and cleaned over 30 cars and was able to raise over Rs.9000.
